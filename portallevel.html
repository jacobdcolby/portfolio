Objective

The goal of this project was to develop a level in an existing game that was playable by the public. The level should have a balance between difficulty and an enjoyable playthrough experience.


Process

While designing the layout of the puzzle, it was much easier to work backward from the exit rather than trying to tie all my ideas into one. This allowed me to create mini-puzzles that all chained together to create the level as a whole.
